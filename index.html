Instructions

Get ready to flex your memory and have a quacking good time with What the Duck?!, a charming card-matching game that's all about adorable ducks. Perfect for all ages, this game features vibrant, high-quality images of ducks that will brighten your day while you test your recall skills. With its simple rules, cheerful visuals, and endless replay value, What the Duck?! is the perfect mix of relaxation and brain-boosting fun.

Your goal is to test your memory by matching pairs of cards. At the start of the game, all cards are laid face down.

  1. Flip Two Cards: On each turn, select and flip over two cards to reveal the duck images.
  2. Match the Ducks: If the two cards have matching images, they stay face-up. If they don't match, they are flipped back face down.
  3. Remember the Ducks: Use your memory to remember the locations of previously revealed duck images to find matches more efficiently.
  4. Win the Game: The game ends when all pairs have been matched. Aim to complete it in the fewest number of moves!

Enjoy the challenge and the charming ducks as you play!
